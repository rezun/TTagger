Following hundreds of streamers on Twitch? TTagger lets you tag, star, and filter them so you always know who's live and why you followed them.

FEATURES
Custom tags - Create color-coded tags like "Music", "Gaming", or "Tech" and assign them to any streamer you follow.
Star your favorites - Mark must-watch streamers so they always appear first.
Live notifications - Get a browser notification when a starred streamer goes live. Optional and easy to toggle on or off.
Filter & sort - Instantly find streamers by name, category, tag, or live status.
Quick-access popup - See who's live at a glance, filtered by tag, without leaving your current page.
Twitch integration - Tag badges and star highlights appear directly on Twitch.tv, so you can organize channels as you browse.
Full dashboard - A dedicated management page with stream previews, viewer counts, uptime, and drag-to-reorder tags.
Export & import - Back up your tags or move them to another browser.
Dark, light & system themes - Matches your preference.
Available in English and German.

PRIVACY
TTagger only connects to the official Twitch API to load your follows list. Your tags and preferences are stored locally in your browser. Nothing is sent to third-party servers.

PERMISSIONS
Storage - Save your tags, preferences, and cached follow data.
Identity - Sign in with your Twitch account via OAuth.
Notifications - Alert you when starred streamers go live (optional).
Alarms - Keep live status up to date in the background.
Twitch.tv access - Display tag badges and star highlights on Twitch pages.
